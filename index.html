Magnus Carlsen

"The Mozart of Chess"

Magnus Carlsen playing at the annual Norway Chess 2021.

Here's a time line of Carlsen's career:

  • 2004 - World's youngest grand master at the time
  • 2005 - Youngest player to be an official World Championship Candidate.
  • 2007 - Reached the semifinal round of the World Chess Cup
  • 2008 - Second place at the World Rapid Chess Championship
  • 2009 - Fifth chess player in the history to achieve an Elo-rating over 2800
  • 2010 - Youngest ever chess player to be ranked World Number One
  • 2011 - highest chess rating ever with 2862
  • 2013 - World Chess Champion, peak rating of 2872
